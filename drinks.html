Drink Recipes

           
Orange Heaven
  • Two parts Cointreau
  • One part Cruzan Coconut rum
  • One part barbancourt
  • One part lime juice
  • One part simple syrup
  • Add brown sugar to taste
  • Add ice, shake, serve strained
Pisco Sour
  • 3 part pisco
  • 1 part lime juice
  • 1 part sugar syrup (janabe de goma)
  • 1 part egg white
  • 3 drops angostura bitter
Potion Magique
  • 1 part lime juie
  • 2 part brown sugar simple syrup
  • 4 part Barbancourt 3 etoiles
  • Shake long and hard
  • Serve strained
Smooth Rita
  • 1 part Lime Juice
  • 1 part simple syrup
  • 2 part Jose Cuervo tequila
  • 1 part cointreau
  • Add ice, shake briefly, serve with the ice
The Beach Bomb
  • 1 Oz Cruzan coconut rum
  • 1 Oz Barbancourt
  • 1 Oz Gin (Tanqueray)
  • 1 Oz Tequila (Hornitos)
  • 1 Oz Simple sirup
  • 1/2 Oz Berry flavored vodka
  • Juice from 1 lime
  • Add ice, shake, serve strained
TSA (The Sunday Afternoon)
  • 1 part lemon juice
  • 1 part agave syrup
  • 3 part Bacardi 151 or equivalent
  • 1 part Citron Vodka
  • 1 part Cruzan Coconut Rum
  • Brown sugar (to taste)
  • Ice - Shake hard. Serve on ice.
